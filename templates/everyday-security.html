Everyday Cybersecurity Tips

15 Essential Tips to Improve Your Cybersecurity:

1. Use strong and unique passwords for each account.
2. Enable two-factor authentication wherever possible.
3. Avoid clicking on suspicious links or downloading unknown attachments.
4. Regularly update your software and security patches.
5. Use a VPN when accessing public Wi-Fi networks.
6. Avoid sharing sensitive information over email or phone unless verified.
7. Keep your devices' operating systems and applications up-to-date.
8. Backup important data regularly and securely.
9. Use a reputable antivirus and malware protection software.
10. Be cautious when connecting external devices like USB drives to your system.
11. Lock your devices when not in use, even at home.
12. Educate yourself on recognizing phishing and scam attempts.
13. Disable unused services and applications on your devices.
14. Secure your home Wi-Fi network with a strong password and encryption.
15. Use a password manager to securely store and generate strong passwords.
